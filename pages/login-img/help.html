Add a Task

  • Choose a meaningful title
  • Select a category
  • Describe the task shortly
  • Select the due date
  • How urgent is the task?
  • Assign the task to a member of your group

    Backlog

  • Your task will be added to the Backlog
  • From here you can pin it on the board

    The Board

  • The title of the task has a background color according to its urgency
  • You can enter a more detailed view of the task by clicking on it
  • In the detailed view you can edit or delete the task and save the changes
  • You can sort the tasks easily by DragnDrop

    Trash

  • Tasks that were deleted from the board appear here
  • You can either delete the task irrevocably or add it back to the board
